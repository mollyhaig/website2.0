50 Iterations

Poster design and animation

Make sure your sound is on!

Assignment Part 1:

Create 50 iterations of a poster.

Assignment Part 2:

Present the posters in a way that relates to their content.

Posters are based on a 1951 concert poster by Josef Müller-Brockman. The animation above combines stop motion with a recording of Alexander Brailowsky playing Chopin's Piano Concerto No. 1 in E minor (the soloist advertised in the original poster playing one of the pieces from the original poster!).
